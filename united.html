United by Blue

Eco-Friendly Essentials for the Holidays

While working on my podcast, I learned about the strain we put on the environment. If you want to do your part to relieve that strain, check out some of these eco-friendly essentials. These durable products from the sustainable company United by Blue will save holiday shopping stress AND the environment, all for a reasonable price. Each product purchased removes one pound of trash from landfills!

Among the company's featured gifts are unique, soft-fabric backpacks made from recycled water bottles, and one-of-a-kind painted mugs available in a variety of patterns. Make sure to also check out their Marled Mariner Beanie ; it is made of 100% recycled polyester and comes in 4 different colors to keep you warm during the winter months.
